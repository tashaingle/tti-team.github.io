Our Commitment

At Bee&Bee Property Management, we aim to provide reliable, friendly and high-quality services to all B&B and short-stay property owners. If something goes wrong or you are unhappy with our service, we want to hear from you so we can put things right quickly.

How to Make a Complaint

You can make a complaint using any of the contact methods below:

  • Email: info@bee&beepropertymanagement.co.uk
  • Phone: 07850 177 876
  • WhatsApp: Chat on WhatsApp

Please include your name, property location, a description of the issue, relevant dates, and how you'd like us to resolve it.
