Our Commitment

Bee&Bee Property Management is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to as many people as possible, including people with disabilities. We aim to follow good practice and design our site so that it is:

  • Clear and easy to navigate
  • Readable on all devices and screen sizes
  • Compatible with screen readers where possible
  • Usable with keyboard-only navigation

What We Have Done

To support accessibility, we have taken steps such as:

  • Using readable fonts and good colour contrast
  • Providing descriptive text for links where needed
  • Using simple, consistent layouts across the site
  • Ensuring forms and buttons are labelled clearly

Some parts of our website include third-party content such as social media icons, embedded links or forms. We cannot guarantee that all third-party content meets the same accessibility standards, but we aim to choose reputable services where possible.
